About Me

Having spent fifty six years living and working on the island of Ireland - I still speak with a posh English accent. I came to study at Trinity College, Dublin in the 60s, and never left.

I've worked in a variety of jobs and come to acting later in life via academia where, as a lecturer in UCD, I learned performance skills were essential for communication with an audience.

Having written and performed a one man show, which went to Edinburgh via the Dublin and Windsor Fringes in the 1990s, I trained in the Meisner Technique and have been pursuing acting work since then.
